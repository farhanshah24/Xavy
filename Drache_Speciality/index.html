Welcome to DRACHE Specialty

Located at Quai au Bois à Brûler 11, in the heart of Brussels, DRACHE Specialty Coffee Bar is dedicated to celebrating exceptional coffee and simple, honest food.
Our passion is sourcing and roasting the finest beans to bring out every delicate aroma in your cup. From rich espresso to slow-brewed filter coffee, each drink is prepared with care and expertise. Pair it with our selection of fresh pastries, artisanal toasts, and light bites made from seasonal ingredients.
Whether you’re starting your day, taking a break, or meeting friends, our warm and modern space invites you to slow down and savour every moment.
Come discover Brussels’ vibrant coffee culture at DRACHE.
