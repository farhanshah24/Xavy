Welcome to Didi & Franc

Located at 296–300 Kennington Road, in the heart of London, Didi & Franc is a cosy café where community and creativity come together over great coffee and fresh, homemade food.
Our menu celebrates simple pleasures: buttery pastries, vibrant salads, artisan sandwiches, and carefully brewed coffees and teas. Whether you’re grabbing a quick breakfast, settling in with your laptop, or meeting friends for a leisurely lunch, our welcoming space is designed to make you feel at home.
Every detail at Didi & Franc reflects our love for quality, warmth, and good conversation. Come by and discover your new favourite spot in Kennington.
